Tirion Fordring

Highlord of the Order of the Silver Hand

He was one of the first five paladins of the Order of the Silver Hand, selected by Archbishop Alonsus Faol along with Uther the Lightbringer, Turalyon, Saidan Dathrohan, and Gavinrad the Dire. A hero of the Alliance of Lordaeron during the Second War,[11] he became Lord of Mardenholde Keep in Hearthglen and held the position for several peaceful years, until he was stripped of his titles, excommunicated of the order and exiled for defending an old orc, Eitrigg.
